The Process

Get Started in 5 Simple Steps

From creating your account to building your first hackathon team — here's exactly how SyncItUp works.

1
Getting Started
Create Your Account

Sign up using your college email address. Enter your name, password, and college name. A one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your email to verify your identity before your account is activated.

OTP Verification College Email Secure Signup
2
Your Profile
Build Your Profile with Skills & Interests

After signing up, complete your profile by listing the skills you bring to a team. Add your interests, availability (full-time, part-time, weekends), and a short bio so others know who you are and what you're looking for.

AI / ML Web Development IoT UI / UX Mobile
3
Discovery
Browse Students from Your College

Head to the Find Teammates page. All profiles shown are from students in your own college — making collaboration natural and trustworthy. Use the skill and domain filters to narrow your search and find the exact kind of teammate you need.

College Filter Skill Filter Domain Filter
4
Connection
Send a Collaboration Request

Found someone with the right skills? Send them a collaboration request in one click. They'll receive a notification and can accept or decline. Once accepted, you're officially connected and can begin chatting immediately.

One-Click Request Accept / Decline Instant Notifications
5
Collaboration
Start Collaborating & Win Together

With your team formed, use the built-in real-time chat to discuss ideas, divide tasks, and plan your hackathon strategy. Your messages are private and stored securely. Now go build something amazing and win that hackathon.

Private Chat Real-Time Messaging Secure History
Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SyncItUp free to use?

Yes, completely free for all college students. No subscription or hidden fees. Sign up and start connecting right away.

Can I connect with students from other colleges?

Currently, the platform focuses on college-scoped connections to maintain trust. Students from your college appear in your feed.

What if I receive an inappropriate request?

You can report users directly. College admins review reports and take action to keep the community safe and professional.

Platform Features

Everything You Need to Build
Your Hackathon Team

SyncItUp is built from the ground up to help college students find the right teammates, collaborate efficiently, and win more hackathons.

Smart Skill-Based Matching

Filter and discover students by the exact skills your project needs — AI/ML, Web Dev, IoT, Mobile, UI/UX, Cybersecurity, and more. No guessing, just the right fit.

College-Specific Network

Browse and connect only with students from your own college. A familiar, trusted network makes collaboration feel natural and safe.

Real-Time Secure Chat

Once connected, chat directly with your teammates. Conversations are private, stored securely, and only accessible between accepted collaborators.

Instant Collaboration Requests

Send and receive collaboration requests in one click. Accept, decline, or view profiles before deciding — no unnecessary friction.

OTP Email Verification

Every account is verified via a one-time password sent to your college email. This keeps the platform authentic and spam-free.

Rich Student Profiles

Showcase your skills, interests, availability, and bio. A well-built profile helps the right teammates discover you without effort.

Project Collaboration Support

Organize your team, assign roles, and keep conversations focused on your project goals — all within the same platform.

Admin Moderation Panel

College-specific admins can review reported users, manage profiles, and ensure community standards are maintained across the network.

Activity & Match Insights

Track your connection requests, chat history, and profile views. Stay informed about who's reaching out and how your network is growing.
